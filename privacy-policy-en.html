Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

TextLater ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Data stored locally

  • Scheduled messages: Your message content and scheduling are stored only on your device
  • Contacts: Contact information is stored locally and never transmitted
  • Preferences: Your app settings (language, date format, etc.)
  • Templates: Your custom message templates

2.2 Usage data (optional)

With your explicit consent, we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage data of app features
  • Technical error reports (without personal content)
  • Usage statistics to improve the application

3. Data Usage

We use your data only to:

  • Operate the application according to your instructions
  • Improve performance and stability
  • Develop new features
  • Provide technical support when needed

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your messages and contacts remain strictly private and never leave your device, except when you explicitly choose to send them via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure local storage on your device
  • No transmission of sensitive data
  • Encryption of anonymous usage data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Refuse or withdraw consent for usage data
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about collected data
  • Export your personal data

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use:

  • System services: SMS, Email, WhatsApp (based on your choice)
  • Analytics: Anonymous data to improve the app (with your consent)

8. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained:

  • Locally on your device until you delete it
  • Anonymous usage data may be retained to improve the application

9. Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be notified through the application.
